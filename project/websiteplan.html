W05 Project: Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

Professional English

The name reflects the school's formal nature and its primary purpose.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide prospective students with a comprehensive overview of the school, its mission, and the range of English courses offered. It serves as a primary point of contact, enabling users to easily find information and connect with the school.

3. Scenarios

What English proficiency level do I need to enroll in a course?

Are there any evening or weekend classes available for working professionals?

4. Color Schema

#FFFFFF (White): Used for the main background and paragraph text areas.

#1C3F95 (Dark Blue): Used for main headings and the primary navigation bar.

#262A2D (Dark Gray): Used for the header, footer, or dark sections of the site.
